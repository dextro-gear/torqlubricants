Speciality Lubricants for

Textile Industry

Torq offers a comprehensive range of specialty lubricants that have been engineered to perform well even under the extremely tough operating environments of the textile industry. Speciality lubricants made by Torq can endure high speed, high temperatures, high loads and vibrations and help extend service life and reduce cost. Designed to surpass conventional lubricants, Torq oils and greases can withstand temperature extremes from -57 °C to 399 °C (-70 °F to 750 °F), while retaining their lubricity, viscosity and chemical integrity.
