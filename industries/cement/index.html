Speciality Lubricants for

Cement Industry

The cement industry is often subject to demanding and tough operating conditions – heavy loads, dusty and corrosive environments, strong vibrations, extreme temperatures, etc. The right lubricant can go a long way in avoiding unnecessary downtime, reducing maintenance time and cost; and increasing efficiency and productivity. Lubricating the machinery of a cement plant is not challenging but it requires experience to understand what type of lubricant can be utilized for the many diverse applications; and the method and frequency in which they are to be used. Torq offers a wide range of high-performance specialty lubricants formulated for the specific needs of the cement industry.
